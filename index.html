About Me


Hi! My name is Nicole. Originally from Connecticut, I currently live in New York City. I graduated from Johnson and Wales University with a Bachelor of Science in International Hotel Management. I have a true passion for travel and this did not stop while working in the hospitality industry. I lived and worked in Florida, North Carolina, New York and Alaska. I currently work in an operations role for an Online Travel Agency supporting over 10,000 properties. In my spare time, I enjoy reading, hiking and of course, coding.
